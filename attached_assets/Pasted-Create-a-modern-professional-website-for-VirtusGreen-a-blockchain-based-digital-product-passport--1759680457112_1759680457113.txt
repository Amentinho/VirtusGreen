**For Users:**
1. **Scan** - Use your camera to scan any product barcode

## Design Requirements:

### Visual Style
- Modern, clean, eco-conscious aesthetic
- Color palette: Primary green (#2D5F3F or similar earth tone), white, light grays, accent colors for CTAs
- Generous white space for clarity
- Rounded corners for modern feel
- Subtle gradients or mesh backgrounds for depth

### Typography
- Clear hierarchy with large, bold headlines
- Sans-serif fonts (Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat)
- Readable body text (16-18px minimum)

### Interactive Elements
- Smooth scroll animations (fade-in, slide-up effects)
- Hover states on all clickable elements
- Sticky navigation bar with transparency effect
- Mobile hamburger menu
- Animated barcode scanning demonstration

### Mobile Responsiveness
- Fully responsive design
- Touch-friendly buttons (minimum 44px height)
- Stacked layouts for mobile
- Optimized images for faster loading
